Atlas / Operative

ARCH 415 — Louisiana Tech University — School of Design


Welcome to the online exhibition portal for ARCH 415! This year's class of fourth-year architecture students participated in the Landscape Architecture Foundation's Green New Deal Superstudio initiative. Students proposed and worked on individual projects throughout the quarter, covering a range of topics addressing decarbonization, justice, and/or jobs.

The course was divided into two halves: Atlas and Operative. Students worked through mapping, imaging, and core sampling to reveal the scales of impact of their chosen topic, and have pressed forward in operative mode to realize one focused opportunity about their topic.

Visitors – click on student names, or explore the work based upon thematic and location tags below.
Students – be so very proud!
