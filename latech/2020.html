ARCH 415 – 2020 SUPERSTUDIO

Louisiana Tech University


Welcome to the online exhibition for ARCH 415 at Louisiana Tech for Fall Quarter 2020. This year's class of fourth-year architecture students participated in the Landscape Architecture Foundation's Green New Deal Superstudio. Students proposed and worked on individual projects throughout the quarter, on a range of topics addressing decarbonization, justice, and/or jobs. Be so very proud! Your faculty, Thomas Provost & Brad Deal
