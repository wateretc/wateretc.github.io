Thomas Provost

M.Arch, RA, NCARB

Assistant Professor of Architecture at The University of Memphis, teaching integrated and capstone design studios, modern architectural history, contemporary theory, architectural research, and professional practice. Throughout, I make space for speculation on architecture as more than service objects. I research the history of design, focusing on resourcefulness, adaptation, and material culture across 21st-C post-industrial landscapes.
