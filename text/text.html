Water.etc

Water.etc is an architecture and design studio that creates environments, visualizations, and conceptual works. He researches dwellings, pre-industrial architecture, minimal structures, gym-floor black markets, and is always interested in collaborating on provocative works, educational forums, preserving material culture, etc. Thomas is a licensed architect in Michigan, Assistant Professor of Architecture at Louisiana Tech University, and also works through sound, collaborative art practices, and mechanical devices.
