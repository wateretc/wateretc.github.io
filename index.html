Thomas Provost is an architect and assistant professor based out of Detroit. Provost founded transdisciplinary studio water.etc in 2017, creating prototypes, furniture, installations, rehabilitations, and conceptual projects. He mentors the next generation of socially-conscious designers and has exhibited and published work on material culture, environmental justice, and the Detroit River.
