Thomas Provost (M.Arch, RA) is an assistant professor of architecture at The University of Memphis. Prior to this, he taught in Louisiana, New York, and Michigan and has worked on a variety of projects at firms in Detroit and Toronto. Collaborating with various disciplines, Provost founded water.etc in 2017, creating prototypes, furniture, installations, renovations, rehabilitations, and small structures. As a registered architect, he mentors the next generation of socially-conscious designers. Provost has exhibited and published work on material culture, environmental justice, and the Detroit River.
