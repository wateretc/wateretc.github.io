Thomas Provost (b. Canada) leads water – a design studio that creates environments, visualizations, and conceptual works. Thomas researches dwellings, minimal structures, gym-floor black markets, and is always interested in collaborating on provocative works, educational forums, preserving material culture, etc. Thomas is a licensed architect in Michigan, Assistant Professor of Architecture at Louisiana Tech, and also works through sound, text, graphic design, collaborative art practices, and mechanical devices.
