Thomas Provost (b. Canada) leads water.etc – a design studio that creates environments, visualizations, and conceptual works. Ongoing design-research projects include pre-fabricated dwellings, various non-invasive structures, and gym floor black markets. We are always interested in collaborating on provocative works, educational forums, preserving material culture, etc. Thomas is a licensed architect in the State of Michigan, Assistant Professor at Louisiana Tech, and also works through sound, text, graphic design, collaborative art practices, and mechanical devices.
