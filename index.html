Water.etc

Water.etc is an architecture and design studio that creates environments, visualizations, and conceptual works. We research dwellings, pre-industrial architecture, resourcefulness, and are always interested in collaborating on provocative works, educational forums, preserving material culture, etc.

Thomas Provost

M.Arch, NCARB

Architect with knowledge of near-death Mojave road trips, old things with wheels, image-making, geography, and sports facts. He is a graduate of McGill and UDM, an itinerant architectural laborer, and assistant professor at Louisiana Tech. Also works on/through sound, collaborative art practices, and mechanical devices.
